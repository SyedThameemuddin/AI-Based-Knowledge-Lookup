Pipeline

How FindX Works

A four-step RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) pipeline under the hood.

📤
Step 1

Upload

Upload your Excel, CSV, or text file to the dashboard.

🔢
Step 2

Embed

Content is converted into 384-dim vectors using SentenceTransformers.

🔍
Step 3

Retrieve

FAISS finds the most semantically similar chunks to your query.

💬
Step 4

Generate

Groq's Llama 3.1 generates a grounded, accurate answer from the retrieved context.
